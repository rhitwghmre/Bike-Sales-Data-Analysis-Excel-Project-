<h2><b>Results & Business Recommendations</b></h2>
<p>
The analysis revealed clear patterns in customer purchasing behavior based on income, age group, commute distance, and demographics.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Middle-aged customers showed higher bike purchase rates</li>
  <li>Higher income groups were more likely to purchase bikes</li>
  <li>Shorter commute distances correlated with higher purchase likelihood</li>
  <li>Regional and demographic differences influenced buying behavior</li>
</ul>

<p>
These insights can help businesses target marketing campaigns, optimize pricing strategies, and improve sales performance.
</p>
